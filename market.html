Retail Gap

There is not a single Micro Center location in the entire state of Oklahoma. The nearest stores require significant travel — through multiple states, across hundreds of miles of highway — for any student or resident who needs tech hardware today, not in three days. This isn't a minor inconvenience. It is a structural gap in retail access for a market of nearly 50,000 people anchored by one of the country's fastest-growing public universities.

Nearest Micro Center Locations

Micro Center operates 25 stores nationwide. The four closest to Stillwater are all out of state, spanning distances that make same-day shopping trips impractical for most students and residents.

Time & Distance Inconvenience
The Cost of Distance

When an OSU student's laptop charger fails the night before a final, or a engineering team's Raspberry Pi stops responding mid-project, the only local option is Amazon with 2–3 day shipping. Best Buy — 45 minutes away in Stillwater's nearest metro area — stocks a limited consumer selection with no dedicated PC components, no GPU selection, and no custom PC building services. The gap between what students need and what is locally available is enormous.

"I needed a specific GPU for my ML project. The closest Micro Center is in Dallas. I either wait four days for shipping or drive seven hours round trip to Texas. I drove." — OSU Computer Science student
What Is Available Locally
🏪

Best Buy — Stillwater, OK

Consumer electronics focus with extremely limited PC component selection. No dedicated GPU wall, no custom PC services, no bulk component inventory. Not a direct replacement for MicroCenter.

📦

Walmart / Amazon Shipping

2–3 day minimum delivery for most components. No ability to inspect before buying. No expert staff. No same-day access. No return counter for immediate exchanges on defective parts.

🚗

Drive to Dallas, TX

210 miles. 3.5 hours each way. $40–60 in gas. A 7-hour round trip plus time in-store. The only way to get same-day access to the full Micro Center product selection from Stillwater today.

What Stillwater Is Missing

A full Micro Center location would bring to Stillwater what currently does not exist anywhere in Oklahoma: a dedicated computer hardware retailer with 25,000+ products, same-day component access, professional PC building services, in-store repair, expert staff with technical depth, and a product breadth that ranges from $3 cables to $5,000 workstation builds. This is the retail gap — and it is completely unoccupied.

🖥️

PC Components

GPUs, CPUs, motherboards, RAM, storage, cases, cooling — the full stack. Nowhere in Oklahoma sells this selection at retail. Students ship or drive.

🔧

Custom PC Building

Micro Center's in-store assembly service is unavailable within 210 miles. OSU students building gaming rigs or lab machines have no professional assembly option locally.

🛠️

In-Store Repair

OEM-certified technicians for hardware repair, virus removal, data recovery, and upgrades. Currently, Stillwater residents drive to OKC or Tulsa for comparable service.

📡

Networking & Lab Gear

Networking hardware, Raspberry Pi, Arduino, microcontrollers, and electronics components for OSU's engineering and CS labs. Currently only available online with lead times.
